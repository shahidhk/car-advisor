The algorithm

The algorith used behind this program compares the total cost of ownership for a petrol and diesel vehicle. If the cost of ownership for a diesel vehicle is less than that of a petrol vehicle even after deducting the amount which can be obtained by investing the initial cost difference as a fixed deposit, Diesel car is recommended. Else a Petrol car is recommended. Below given are the exact steps followed in the algorithm.
  • The initial cost difference is calculated.
  • Total running cost is calculated for each variety. Which includes
    • Total maintenance cost per kilometer multiplied by the total number of kms driven during the period of ownership
    • Total running cost (fuel cost) for the period of ownership
  • Total cost of ownership is calculated after reducing the resale value of each car
  • The initial investment is cosidered as a fixed deposit for the years ownership and that amound after adding interest is deducted from the petrol car's cost of ownership
  • If the resulting amount is greater than zero, diesel is more profitable, and the gains start to show up when the specific mark is reached on odo
  • If it is negative, Petrol iwill be the better option
